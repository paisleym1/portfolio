Paisley Maschmeier's Assignment 4: Subtractive Manufacturing

For this assignment I will make a lamp similar to the one I made before, except this time I will use Grasshopper to design the pentagons. When I made my lamp before, I had to use hot glue to connect the pentagons and it took a lot of time. For this assignment, I will make it so each pentagon piece can snap together using fingerjoints/boxjoints, tabs, or another joining method. When complete, the pentagons will made made a dodecahedran shape and will hang on a light.
