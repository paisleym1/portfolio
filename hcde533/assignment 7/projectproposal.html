Paisley Maschmeier's Assignment 4: Subtractive Manufacturing

For this assignment I will make a lamp similar to the one I made before, except this time I will use Grasshopper to design the pentagons. In assignment six (Lamps!) I used hot glue to connect the pentagons and it took a lot of time to assemble. For this assignment, I will make it so each pentagon piece can snap together using fingerjoints/boxjoints, tabs, or another joining method. I will accompish this combining the puzzle box from assignment 3 and the lamp from assignment 7. When complete, the pentagons will make up a dodecahedran shape and will hang on a light.
