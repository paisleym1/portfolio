Paisley Maschmeier's Assignment 4: Subtractive Manufacturing

For this assignment I will make a lamp similar to the one I made for assignment 6, except this time I will use Grasshopper to design the pentagons. In assignment six (Lamps!) I used hot glue to attach the pentagons to eachother and it took a lot of time to assemble. For this assignment, I will make it so each pentagon interlocks with another and clasps together using fingerjoints/boxjoints, tabs, or another joining method. I will accompish this combining the interlocking pieces box from assignment 7 and the lamp from assignment 7. When complete, the pentagons will make up a dodecahedran shape and will hang on a light.

Techniques

  • Rhino Modeling
  • Grasshopper
  • 3d Printing
  • Interference Fits
  • Incorporating Stock Parts

The lamp will be used create light in a fun and cool looking way. You will be able to take is places easily because it can deconstruct into smaller, storable pieces.
