Paisley Maschmeier's Assignment 1: Modelling and Laser Cutting

For this assignment I wanted to make a pieces that look like ninja stars

I started by using my digital calibers to measure the thickness of the cardboard I intended to laser cut. I measured the cardboard to be 3.0 mm thick, or 0.12 inches.

I decided to measure millimeter measurement for my 3d model in Rhino because I will use Inkscape to export the model to a vector illustration, and Inkscape uses millimeters.

Next I needed to design the model of my piece in Rhino. I decided to use the millimeter measurement for my 3d model because I planned on using Inkscape to export the model into a vector illustration, and Inkscape uses millimeters. Download my model here: Ninja Star Model
